Girl Develop It San Jose - Teaching Materials

This site hosts a variety of CC-licensed web development curriculum which was developed for Girl Develop It SJ as well as conference trainings. This curriculum is designed to be taught, but it may be useful to self-learners as well.

Interested in teaching any of it?

  • Consult our teaching guide.
  • Try doing the exercises yourself and make sure you understand what issues students might run into while doing them.
  • After you teach it, report issues and open pull requests on the gdisf/teaching-materials Github repo.
  • Let us know about what you taught where.

Interested in contributing? Fork our curricula on Github!
